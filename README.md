# My Project

## R1
The system shall allow students to register for courses from the official registration start date until the registration deadline set by the university. Late registration shall be permitted for 3 calendar days after the deadline with a late registration fee of Rs. 1,000.

## R2
The system shall display the total seat capacity and available seats for each course. If all seats are filled, students shall not be directly enrolled but may join the waitlist.

## R3
The system shall send an email confirmation within 1 minute of successful course registration, waitlist enrollment, or course drop. The confirmation shall include course name, section, schedule, and credit hours.

## R4
The system shall prevent students from registering for a course unless all prerequisites are completed. If an instructor grants approval, registration shall be allowed only after the approval is recorded in the system.

## R5
The system shall prevent students from registering for courses with overlapping schedules, except when one of the courses is classified as a fully asynchronous online course.

## R6
The system shall limit students to a maximum of 18 credit hours per semester. Students may register for up to 24 credit hours only after advisor approval is recorded in the system.

## R7
The system shall allow students to drop courses until the end of Week 6 of the semester. A drop fee of Rs. 2,000 per course shall apply to drops made after Week 2. Course drops shall not be allowed after Week 6.

## R8
The system shall support a waitlist for full courses. When a seat becomes available, the system shall automatically enroll the first student on the waitlist in first-in, first-out order, provided the student does not exceed credit limits or have schedule conflicts.
